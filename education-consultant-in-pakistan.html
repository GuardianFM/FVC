Mission Statement

“Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world” - Nelson Mandela

Education is quite simply a key to unlock meaningful opportunities on the path to success. Our mission is to provide students all over the world the opportunity to access this powerful tool to elevate their lives and of those around them.

About Us:

Our goal at Phoenix Consulting Group is to foster unity and understanding between people across the globe by creating opportunities for students, regardless of their origin, to study and experience international cultures while gaining knowledge to affect positive change.

Our Story:

The entire PCG team started their professional journey as international students. We understand, first hand, the complicated process of first applying for admissions, securing international visas, arranging for lodging and surviving in an unfamiliar location. Therefore, we are dedicated to assisting hopeful students by creating a more streamlined and accessible study abroad admissions process.

Message from CEO

At the age of 19, I left my home country to pursue higher education in the UK. While studying for my MBA from University of Wales, I counseled international students and assisted them in navigating the ins and outs of the international education process. I always had a passion for helping people and after graduation, I joined Morgan Marks Solicitors to direct my energies towards helping the immigrant community in UK with legal issues. I then moved on to work at one of the biggest firms, WPP, as an accountant. As my international education allowed me to live a successful life, I am dedicated to making the study abroad process more accessible to prospective students all over the world.

To this end, I quit my successful career as an accountant and started PCG to fulfill my life’s purpose of providing a one-stop shop platform where hopeful students have my guidance and expertise at their disposal to select the perfect institute, the perfect course and the perfect study abroad destination that meets their needs.
